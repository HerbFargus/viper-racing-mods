author: Franco Fusco
Beta Release, Revision 0.34
24-bit Targa

How to use:

1. Place "Stp2Tga.exe" in the same folder as your rescracked .stp files from Viper Racing.

2. Double click the stp2tga.bat file and all .stp files will be converted to .tga files.

3. Modify or change the tga files ( keeping dimensions the same size ) of your choice
and convert them back to .stp files with the tga2stp.exe and tga2stp.bat files included.

Cheers!
